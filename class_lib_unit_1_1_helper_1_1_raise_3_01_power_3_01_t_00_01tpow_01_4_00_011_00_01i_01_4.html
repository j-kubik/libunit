template<typename T, int tpow, int i>
class LibUnit::Helper::Raise< Power< T, tpow >, 1, i >

Helper class used for raising to given power classes representing units and dimensions.

Specialization for power of one and Power class. It is necesary to avoid ambiguity when selcting template specialization. Used to skip Power wrapper class if it's not necesary.
